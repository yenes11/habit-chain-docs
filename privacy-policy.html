Habit Chain

Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 29, 2026  ·  Last updated: May 29, 2026

Your privacy matters. Habit Chain is designed to store your personal habit data locally on your device. We collect the minimum information necessary to provide the service.

1. Who We Are

Habit Chain is developed and operated by Enes Yurtsever. If you have any privacy-related questions, you can reach us at ey.enesyurtsever@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

2a. Information stored locally on your device

The following data is stored exclusively on your device using a local SQLite database and is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Your display name (entered during onboarding)
  • Habits you create (name, icon, color, reminder time)
  • Daily habit completion records and streak data
  • Streak freeze records
  • App settings (theme, language preference)

2b. Information collected by third-party services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data on our behalf:

Service Purpose Data collected
RevenueCat In-app subscription management Anonymous device ID, purchase receipts, subscription status. No personal identity data is shared.
Apple App Store / Google Play Payment processing for Premium subscriptions Handled entirely by Apple / Google under their own privacy policies.
Expo / EAS App delivery and over-the-air updates May collect anonymous crash reports and device metadata for stability purposes.

3. Notifications

Habit Chain may request permission to send you local push notifications as daily habit reminders. These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device and do not involve any data leaving your device. You can turn off notifications at any time in your device Settings.

4. How We Use Information

We use the information described above solely to:

  • Provide and personalise the App's habit tracking features
  • Process and manage your Premium subscription
  • Send you habit reminder notifications (with your permission)
  • Improve app stability and performance through anonymous crash data

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your habit data is stored locally on your device in an encrypted SQLite database managed by Expo SQLite. We do not have access to your habit data. Because data is stored locally, it may be lost if you delete the App, reset your device, or do not use device backups. We recommend enabling iCloud or Google backups on your device to protect your data.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access and portability: You can view all your habit data directly within the App.
  • Deletion: You can delete all your data at any time via Settings → Reset All Data. Uninstalling the App also removes all locally stored data.
  • Subscription data: For data held by RevenueCat, Apple, or Google, please refer to their respective privacy policies or contact them directly.

For any other privacy requests, contact us at ey.enesyurtsever@gmail.com.

7. Data Retention

Since all habit data is stored locally on your device, it is retained for as long as the App is installed. Subscription-related data (e.g., purchase receipts) is retained by RevenueCat for as long as required for transaction processing and fraud prevention.

8. International Users

Habit Chain is available internationally. If you are located outside Turkey, please be aware that any data processed by our third-party services (such as RevenueCat) may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. By using the App, you consent to such transfers as necessary to provide the service.

9. Third-Party Links

The App does not contain links to third-party websites. Any third-party services used (see Section 2b) operate under their own privacy policies.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
